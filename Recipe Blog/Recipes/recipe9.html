Quinoa Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients:

  • 4 large bell peppers
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove seeds and membranes.
  3. Rinse quinoa under cold water. In a saucepan, bring vegetable broth to a boil. Add quinoa, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes or until quinoa is cooked.
  4. In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, black beans, corn, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Stir in 1/2 cup of shredded cheese.
  5. Stuff the bell peppers with the quinoa mixture and place in a baking dish. Drizzle with olive oil.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, until peppers are tender.
  7. Sprinkle remaining cheese on top and bake for an additional 5 minutes, until cheese is melted.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve.
