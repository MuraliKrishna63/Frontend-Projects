Spinach and Feta Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped spinach and cook until wilted.
  4. Pour the eggs into the skillet and cook until they start to set.
  5. Sprinkle feta cheese over half of the omelette.
  6. Fold the omelette in half and continue cooking until the eggs are fully set and the cheese is melted.
  7. Serve hot.
