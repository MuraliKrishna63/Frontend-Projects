Chickpea Salad Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise or Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 stalks celery, finely chopped
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Bread or sandwich rolls, for serving
  • Lettuce, tomato slices, avocado slices (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, mash chickpeas with a fork or potato masher.
  2. Add mayonnaise or Greek yogurt, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, celery, and green onions. Stir until well combined.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Spread chickpea salad onto bread or sandwich rolls. Top with lettuce, tomato slices, and avocado slices if desired.
  5. Serve immediately.
