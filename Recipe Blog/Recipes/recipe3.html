Lentil Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried lentils
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse lentils under cold water.
  2. In a large pot, combine lentils, water or vegetable broth, onion, garlic, curry powder, diced tomatoes, and coconut milk.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes or until lentils are tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with rice or naan.
