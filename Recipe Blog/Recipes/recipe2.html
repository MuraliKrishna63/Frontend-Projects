Black Bean Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans (15 oz each) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, and bell pepper. Cook until vegetables are softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add black beans, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  3. Bring soup to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes.
  4. Use an immersion blender to blend soup until smooth, or transfer half of the soup to a blender and blend until smooth. Return blended soup to the pot and stir to combine.
  5. Adjust seasoning if needed. Serve hot.
