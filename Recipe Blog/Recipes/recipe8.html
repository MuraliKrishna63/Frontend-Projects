Black Bean Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  2. Add black beans, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Warm tortillas in a separate pan or microwave.
  4. Spoon black bean mixture onto tortillas. Top with avocado slices, salsa, and fresh cilantro.
  5. Serve immediately.
